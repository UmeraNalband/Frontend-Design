Admission

Admission Process Overview

Winkel Twinkel Preschool welcomes new families with an easy admission process. Start by submitting an inquiry through our website or phone to learn about our programs. Next, meet with our Head of Preschool to discuss your child’s needs and ask questions. Afterward, collect the admission form, which can be obtained in person or via email. Once completed, pay the registration fee to secure your child's spot. Finally, we’ll confirm admission and send a welcome packet with essential details about orientation and what to expect. We’re excited to have your child join the Winkel Twinkel family!

Curriculum

Our Curriculum

Our curriculum nurtures children's curiosity and creativity, tailored to their individual learning styles. Inspired by Montessori, Reggio Emilia, and Howard Gardner’s theory of multiple intelligences, we create a hands-on, play-based environment. Children engage in self-directed learning, exploring and expressing themselves freely, while our educators guide them through personalized, age-appropriate activities that blend digital tools with natural experiences. Aligned with Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) standards, our program supports development through play, encompassing sensorial exploration, early literacy, numerical concepts, and creative arts. We foster a nurturing environment that empowers children to take charge of their learning journey, building lifelong confidence and a love for learning.
